First Project- Health Care Agency Application

Designed and developed Health Care Agency Application using Asp.NET and C# which provided its users a convenient mean to book medical appointments online using any browser with any doctor who had chosen to get registered within the application earlier

Guided a group of three peers and finalized most efficient & easiest manner to perform the booking task

Captured a number of user-oriented features, such as, allowing user to ask doctors to come to patients’ homes and allowing user to schedule the timeslot in advance to save patient’s time

Documented a journal report including detailed user manual
